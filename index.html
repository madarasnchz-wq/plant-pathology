Check what you observe on the plant — location, appearance, color, and conditions. The index cross-references your marks against 38 crop diseases spanning fungi, bacteria, viruses, phytoplasmas, oomycetes, and algae — including diseases common to Sri Lankan tea, rubber, coconut, rice, and spice crops — and ranks the closest matches. Each result is labeled by pathogen type. Not a substitute for lab confirmation on high-value crops.
